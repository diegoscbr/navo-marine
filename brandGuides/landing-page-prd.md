# NAVO Marine Technologies

## Landing Page Product Requirements Document (PRD)

## 1. Overview
- **Product:** NAVO Marine Technologies Marketing Website
- **Page Type:** Primary Landing Page (Homepage)
- **Audience:** High-performance sailing teams, race organizers, yacht clubs, performance sailors, federations, marine technology partners
- **Primary Goal:** Establish NAVO as the premier marine technology and race intelligence partner in high-performance sailing.

## 2. Brand Foundation
### Brand Name
- NAVO Marine Technologies
- Short form: NAVO Marine

### Positioning
NAVO Marine Technologies is a performance-first marine technology company specializing in:
- Official Brand Partner of Vakaros Atlas II
- Race management services
- High-performance sailing analytics
- Advanced marine data systems
- Official Premier Partner of UR SAILING

NAVO bridges hardware, analytics, and race execution into a unified performance ecosystem.

## 3. Tagline Direction
### Primary Recommended Tagline (Tech + Authority Focus)
- Technology That Moves Sailing Forward.

### Alternate Options
- Precision for Performance.
- Innovation at Sea.
- Elevating Marine Performance.

## 4. Strategic Positioning
### NAVO Should Feel
- Elite
- Technical
- Performance-driven
- Clean
- Modern
- Oceanic but not cliche
- Stripe.dev clarity meets Saildrone authority

### Avoid
- Nautical cliches
- Overly recreational tone
- Yacht-lifestyle branding
- Overuse of stock imagery

## 7. Visual Direction
### Color Palette
**Primary**
- Deep Navy (#0B1F2A)
- Midnight Blue (#0F2C3F)
- Electric Marine Blue (#1E6EFF)

**Accent**
- Cyan glow highlights
- Subtle gradients

### Typography
- Sans-serif (Inter, Neue Haas Grotesk, or similar)
- Clean, modern, slightly condensed

### Design Principles
- Large negative space
- No clutter
- Grid precision
- Subtle motion
- Data overlays
- Dark mode dominant

## 8. UX Principles
- Fast load
- Minimal scrolling friction
- Clear hierarchy
- Smooth section transitions
- Subtle micro-animations
- Data visualizations feel real, not decorative

## 9. Technical Requirements
- Built with performance-first framework (Next.js or equivalent)
- Responsive (mobile-first)
- Optimized image handling
- SEO structured data
- Analytics integration
- CRM form capture

## 13. Competitive Benchmarking
**Stripe.dev**
- Clean technical confidence
- Clear modular sections
- Developer-grade clarity

**Saildrone**
- Strong authority positioning
- High-end visuals
- Ocean + technology balance

**Regent Craft**
- Futuristic
- Bold statements
- Premium feel

NAVO should sit between Saildrone's authority and Stripe's clarity.

## 14. Success Metrics
- Time on page > 1:45
- Inquiry form conversion > 3%
- Bounce rate < 50%
- Direct partnership inquiries from elite teams

## 15. Final Brand Expression
NAVO is not a sailing lifestyle company.

NAVO is a marine performance technology company. It exists to:
- Increase speed
- Improve decision-making
- Professionalize race execution
- Modernize competitive sailing
